About Pedlar Street

Quality You Can Trust Since 2010

Pedlar Street is a leading exporter of premium Ceylon cinnamon and spices from Sri Lanka. We have built our reputation on delivering exceptional quality products to international buyers across Europe, North America, Asia, and beyond.

Our commitment to excellence begins at the source. We work directly with local farmers and harvesters in Sri Lanka's prime spice-growing regions to ensure we receive only the finest raw materials.

Every batch undergoes rigorous quality control testing for moisture content, microbial counts, and authenticity to meet international standards including ISO 22000, HACCP, and FDA compliance.

Whether you're a food manufacturer, distributor, or specialty retailer, we provide bulk quantities with flexible packaging options and competitive pricing for wholesale partners.
